ICSET is a premier forum for the ICTAK paper presentation contest, where the presenters can exhibit their research and innovation skills in the field of skills, engineering, technology and employability. As the importance of research is growing as fast as the rapidly changing and challenging world. The main purpose of the research is to get deep into the topic so that something helpful can be churned out which can be used by everybody.

This paper presentation contest is aimed at developing scientific temper among the scholars and also to provide an opportunity to present their original scientific investigation to the public. The paper presentation is aligned to the conference theme and subthemes, its application in the society.
